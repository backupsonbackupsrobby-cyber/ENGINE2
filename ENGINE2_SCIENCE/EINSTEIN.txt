ALBERT EINSTEIN — SPACETIME & FIELD THEORY SPECIALIST

Developed special relativity, general relativity, and mass–energy equivalence (E = mc²).
Explained the photoelectric effect and provided evidence for atoms via Brownian motion.

KEY FACTS
- Born: 14 March 1879, Ulm
- Died: 18 April 1955, Princeton
- Height: 170 cm
- Eyes: Brown
