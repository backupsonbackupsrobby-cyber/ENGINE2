NIKOLA TESLA — ELECTRICAL & RESONANCE SPECIALIST

Inventor and electrical engineer whose work reshaped the technological foundation of the modern world.
Developed AC power systems, AC induction motor, and Tesla coil. Explored resonance, wireless control,
and high-voltage transmission.
